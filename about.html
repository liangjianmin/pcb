One-stop solution for PCB manufacturing and PCB assembly PCBBUY provides printed circuit board (PCB) prototype at quick turn times & full / partial turnkey PCB assembly services worldwide. We We have provided high quality PCBs to numerous companies in a broad spectrum of industries, from consumer electronics to telecommunications, new energy, instrument, automotive and more.

As fast as 24hours we provide customers with ultra-fast2 layers PCB prototype service within 24 hours.

Competitive price Have no hidden price, have no expedited fee,we don’t have the lowest price,but the best price with high quality.

One-year warranty Highest quality: we are certified with ISO9001 and UL. All our PCBs are produced and inspected under IPC class 2 standard.

MES (Manufacturing Execution System) PCBBUY relies on the manufacturing execution system that connects and monitors machines and work centers on the factory floor.
